WordScramble

A simple iPhone game built with SwiftUI in XCode 11. Majority of the design is from the "100 Days of SwiftUI" tutorials from hackingwithswift.com, with some minor modifications.

You are given an eight-letter word and must come up with new words that can be made up from the original word's letters. Words must be at least 3 letters long, and actually be real words!
